Send an SR1 form

The SR1 form has replaced the DS1500 form. You can use it to support your patient applying for a benefit under the Special Rules.

Use this service to tell the Department of Work and Pensions (DWP) that you have a patient who:

  • has a progressive disease and
  • you would not be surprised if they were to live for less than 12 months

What you will need

You will need to have the patient's:

  • National Insurance number
  • diagnosis and other relevant conditions
  • date of diagnosis
  • clinical features that indicate a severe progressive condition
  • treatment plan
  • date that they have met the criteria under special rules

If you are using the service for the first time

You will need to complete an account profile. Your profile will be automatically used on every SR1 form you complete.

To complete your profile, you need your:

  • professional registration number
  • mobile number
  • organisation name and address

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).
