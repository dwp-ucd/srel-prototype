What medical evidence we ask for

The SR1 form asks for medical evidence of:

  • diagnosis/diagnoses and other relevant conditions
  • date of diagnosis
  • whether the patient is aware of their diagnosis and prognosis
  • clinical features that indicate a severe progressive condition (for example, investigation results, staging if appropriate)
  • information about treatment that has been received, is ongoing or is planned

You do not need to give a specific prognosis. Please use language that you would normally use when communicating with other clinicians.
