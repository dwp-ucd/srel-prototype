Before you start

It usually takes between 6 to 12 minutes to complete a SR1 form. You cannot save your information and come back later.

If you do not enter anything into the form for more than 15 minutes, you will be automatically signed out. This means your answer will be deletedand you will need to start again. This is to protect your and the patient’s information.

What you will need

The SR1 form asks for medical evidence of:

  • diagnosis and other relevant conditions
  • date of diagnosis
  • date that they have met the criteria under Special Rules
  • clinical features that inicare a severe progressive condition
  • treatment plan

You do not need to give a specific prognosis. Please use language that you would normally use when communicating with other clinicians.

You will also need to tell us the patient’s:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • address
  • National Insurance number

The online service is currently only available to NHS England.

If you are regitered with NHS Scotland, NHS Wales or Health and Social Care in Northern Ireland you can still send a SR1 form by email or post (opens in new tab).
