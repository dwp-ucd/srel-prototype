How to send an online SR1 form

To send an online SR1 form, you will need an account.

In your account, you can:

  • create a profile to add your details to your SR1 forms
  • save incomplete SR1 forms
  • set reminders for incomplete SR1 forms
  • view, download, and print completed SR1 forms

What you will need

You will need to tell us your patientâ€™s:

  • National Insurance number
  • diagnosis and other relevant conditions
  • date of diagnosis
  • date that they have met the criteria under special rules
  • clinical features that indicate a severe progressive condition
  • treatment plan

If you are using the service for the first time

You must create your profile when you sign in for the first time.

To complete your profile, you need your:

  • professional registration number
  • mobile number
  • organisation name and address

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).
