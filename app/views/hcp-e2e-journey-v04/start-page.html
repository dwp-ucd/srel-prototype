How to tell DWP that your patient is eligible for the Special Rules for end of life

Skip to contents of guide
The SR1 form has replaced the DS1500 form. You can use it to support your patient applying for a benefit under the Special Rules.

Use this service to tell the Department of Work and Pensions (DWP) that you have a patient who:

  • has a progressive disease and
  • you would not be surprised if they were to live for less than 12 months

What you will need

You will need to tell us your patient’s:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • address
  • National Insurance number
  • diagnosis and other relevant conditions
  • date of diagnosis
  • date that they have met their criteria under special rules
  • clinical features that inicare a severe progressive condition
  • treatment plan

Your account

To send an online SR1 form, you will need an account.

In your account, you can:

  • create a profile to add your details to your SR1 forms
  • save incomplete SR1 forms
  • set reminders for incomplete SR1 forms
  • view, download, and print completed SR1 forms

If you are using the service for the first time

You must create your profile when you sign in for the first time.

To complete your profile, you need your:

  • professional registration number
  • mobile number
  • organisation name and address

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).
